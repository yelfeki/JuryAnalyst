4
Stage 4 — Voir Dire Strategy +
The Questions That Matter
Attitudinal predictors become targeted voir dire probes — designed to surface the juror biases that demographics alone would miss.
Probe — Tort Reform Stance
"Some people feel that large jury awards help hold wrongdoers accountable. Others feel they drive up costs for everyone. How do you generally see it?"
Intent: surfaces damage cap sympathizers early
Probe — Causation Openness
"If a doctor says an injury is related to an event, but another doctor disagrees, how would you think through that conflict?"
Intent: reveals skepticism toward medical causation
Probe — Empathy / Just World
"When someone experiences a serious setback, how much do you think their own choices played a role?"
Intent: uncovers victim-blaming orientation
Probe — Invisible Injury Acceptance
"How do you evaluate injuries that don't show up on imaging or tests but are described in detail by the person experiencing them?"
Intent: critical for TBI and soft-tissue cases
