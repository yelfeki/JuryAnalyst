How this connects to Jury Analyst
This simulator runs on the same attitudinal data our Big Data Study generates
The predictors in this model — tort reform beliefs, lawsuit attitudes, personal injury experience, causation skepticism — are the exact variables measured in a Jury Analyst Big Data Study survey. The Jury Simulator then takes those profiles and runs deliberation-weighted outcome modeling. What you see here is the live version of that process: real attitudinal data, translated into expected case value before voir dire begins.
